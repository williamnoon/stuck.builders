# Project tagging skill

How to tag projects Will hasn't classified yet.

## Rules

- **Never guess.** Ask Will.
- **Ask about at most 3 at a time.** Don't overwhelm.
- **One-liner ask.** "bitsbots + evenanayah + viralcar — for each, is it business, experiment, with-sons, or infra?"
- Once he answers, call `tag_project` for each. Then move on. Don't dwell.

## The tags

- **business** — has customers, revenue, or explicit business plan. (stuck.builders is business.)
- **experiment** — exploratory, no customers, may not go anywhere. That's fine.
- **with-sons** — Will works on this with or for one of his sons. First-class weight. Never surfaced as debt.
- **infra** — his own tools (radar, jarvis, personal utilities).
- **archived** — deliberately paused. Don't surface unless he asks directly.

## When in doubt

If Will says "not sure" for a project, tag it `experiment` for now. He can retag later. Never leave a project untagged after asking about it.
