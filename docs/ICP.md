# ICP — stuck.builders

One canonical ICP for The Build (virtual + LIVE). Two secondary segments feed the funnel and the back-end.

---

## Primary ICP — The Build customer

**Who:** non-technical business owner, 30–55, running a real income-earning business today. Not a hobby, not pre-revenue.

**Industries** (from live `/build-b`):
1. Real Estate
2. E-commerce
3. Trades & Construction
4. Coaching & Fitness
5. Marketing Agencies
6. Finance & Mortgage
7. Health & Wellness
8. Beauty & Salons
9. Hospitality
10. Professional Services

**Pain (their words, not ours):**
- "Everything runs through me."
- "My tools don't talk to each other — Gmail, Xero, CRM, Shopify are five separate universes."
- "I keep starting AI stuff and stalling. I've tried ChatGPT, tried Claude, tried a Zapier thing, none of it stuck."
- "I know AI can do this, I just can't figure out how to actually wire it into my day."
- "Evenings are gone to admin catch-up."

**Dream:** one AI operating system that sees my business, acts inside it, and runs the repetitive work — so I'm no longer the bottleneck. Hands-on-their-keyboard, walk out running.

**Tools they already use** (see `/build-b` list): Gmail, Xero, HubSpot, Stripe, Slack, Notion, Google Cal, Shopify, QuickBooks, Airtable, Calendly, +24 more supported.

**AI models they've touched:** Claude, GPT-4o, Gemini, Copilot.

**What they've tried and it didn't stick:** courses, YouTube, ChatGPT prompts, Zapier, "AI consultants" who never installed anything.

**What they need:** someone to sit next to them, install the environment, wire their real tools, and unstick them the moment they stall.

---

## Not a fit (explicit)

- Wants done-for-you. Not our model — they build, Will unsticks. Refer them elsewhere.
- Agency-shopping / RFP mode. Not consulting. One session, one system.
- Pre-revenue / no business yet. Nothing to install AI into.
- Wants a course, a subscription, or a community as the primary deliverable. Ship Club exists but only as a back-end.
- Needs custom software built for them. This is AI installation on top of existing tools, not custom dev.

## ICP language rules for copy + ads

- Speak in their vocabulary: "leads," "quotes," "invoices," "clients," "bookings" — not "workflows," "pipelines," "orchestration."
- Name the tools they actually use.
- Never call them "founders" or "builders" — they're operators.
- Never assume technical fluency. If a term needs a footnote, cut the term.
